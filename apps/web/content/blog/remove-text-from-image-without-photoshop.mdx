## Tips for Getting the Best Results

- **Use high-resolution source images.** More pixel data around the text means better reconstruction.
- **Choose the right mode.** Use "Remove Specific Text" when you only want some text gone.
- **Check complex backgrounds.** Solid backgrounds reconstruct almost perfectly. Detailed areas behind text may need a second pass.
- **PNG for best quality.** RemoveTexts accepts JPG, PNG, and WebP up to 10 MB.
